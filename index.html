Did you know 65% of runners get injured every year?

The general consensus amongst leisure runners is that running is simple. All you need are a pair of shoes.

However, the facts say otherwise -

65 % of runners get injured every year and
40 % of injuries are knee related.

We wanted to change this, by making people focus on their Running Cadence.

Wait..What is even Running Cadence?

Running Cadence – also known as stride rate – is the number of steps a runner takes per minute (SPM). It’s the most common metric because the shorter your stride length and the quicker your stride rate, the faster and better you run.

With our wearable Kadenz, we will make sure that your form and technique improves since your feet will land underneath you – in your center of gravity.

How will Kadenz wearables help improve this technique?

Kadenz's latest haptic feedback technology uses advanced vibration patterns and waveforms to convey information to a runner.

With our little piece of technology, Kadenz will give progressive and personalised advice on your running technique and help you reach your ideal running form.
