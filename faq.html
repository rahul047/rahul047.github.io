FAQ

How often do I have to charge the battery?

The average charge lasts for over 5 hours and it takes around 1 hour to charge.

Where can I wear it?

During product testing we found that the best experience is when you wear around your wrist or arm!

Is the Kadenz waterproof?

The Kadenz is designed to be sweatproof. However, please note that your Kadenz is not designed to be waterproof so any exposure to water, especially submersion, will likely damage it.

Do you offer warranty?

Yes. All kadenz are covered for 2 years.

Can I return the kadenz?

Yes. You can return the kadenz in 14 days after receiving it. The kadenz must be in its original condition for full refund. Please note that have to pay the return postage yourself.
